As a developer, I remind myself everyday that my role in life is to “develop”. If I considered everything that I’ve ever worked on as completed, then my career title would make the least amount of sense, and should be changed to “finisher”. Whether it be a project that’s started or ourselves as people living day to day life. there always be something that we need to improve. This is why I’m passionate about what I do, because everyday I get a new opportunity to learn how to better myself in my career path and my life, while helping others do the same. It’s more than a job, it’s a lifestyle.
